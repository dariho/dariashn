Darías Holgado

Cognitive effort, exercise performance, and evidence synthesis.

I study the cognitive and neural bases of exercise and performance. My work bridges Sport Science, Psychology, Cognitive Neuroscience, and Statistics to generate robust evidence and practical impact.

Projects

Current lines of work.

The project themes are adapted from the existing project page and tied to open resources when available.

Cognitive effor and physical exercise

  • How mental fatigue impacts exercise, how exercise impacts mental fatigue, and how to delay the onset of mental fatigue.
  • Assessing the evidential value of the current literature of mental fatigue and exercise
  • Individualized mental fatigue does not impact neuromuscular function and exercise performance
  • The effects of mental fatigue on objective and subjective indexes of sport performance
  • Conceptual replication of "Mental fatigue impairs physical performance in humans"

Transcranial direct current stimulation

  • Can we actually enhance exercise performance by stimulating the brain? The current evidence does not seem to support this notion.
  • Meta-analysis of the effects of tDCS on exercise performance
  • Umbrella review of the effects of tDCS on exercise performance

Analgesics and exercise performance

  • Can analgesics like paracetamol, tramadol, or glucocorticoids improve exercise performance?
  • Network meta-analysis of analgesics to improve exercise performance
  • Tramadol and exercise and cognitive performance
  • Paracetamol and exercise and cognitive performance
