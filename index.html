Darías Holgado

Cognitive effort, exercise performance, and evidence synthesis.

I study the subjective experience of exercise and cognitive performance. My work bridges Sport Science, Psychology, Cognitive Neuroscience, and Statistics to generate robust evidence and practical impact.

Transcranial direct current stimulation

Completed projects

  • Transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS) over the left prefrontal cortex does not affect time-trial self-paced cycling performance: Evidence from oscillatory brain activity and power output
